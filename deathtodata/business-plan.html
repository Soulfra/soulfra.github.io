Market Analysis

The search engine market is highly competitive, but there's a growing demand for privacy-focused options. As users become increasingly aware of online tracking and data collection, they're seeking alternatives that prioritize their online security. Our target audience consists of individuals who value their digital privacy and are willing to engage with a platform that respects their data.

According to recent surveys, over 70% of internet users are concerned about online tracking, and over 50% would switch to a privacy-focused search engine if given the option. The market size for privacy-focused search engines may be small now, but it has significant growth potential as more users join the movement against surveillance capitalism.

  • DeathToData potential: $10B+ addressable market

Our competitive advantages include:

By partnering with like-minded organizations, we can ensure our search engine not only protects user data but also supports a broader movement towards online freedom and transparency.

Revenue Strategy

Our business model is built on sustainable, ethical revenue streams that don't rely on surveillance:

  • Privacy-first ads - Contextual, not behavioral (no tracking)
  • Premium features - Advanced search filters, API access
  • Partnerships - Privacy-focused companies and organizations
  • VIBES ecosystem - Transaction fees on marketplace

Our pricing strategy balances revenue needs with user affordability. The free version will be available to all users, while the paid subscription will target power users who require advanced search filters and other premium features.
