About Soulfra

Soulfra is a self-sovereign publishing platform built on the principle that you own your identity, your data, and your voice.

In a world of centralized platforms that control, censor, and monetize your content, Soulfra offers an alternative: a decentralized, open-source system where your writing lives in a database you control, and your published content is broadcast to the world like Bitcoin transactions.

Your keys. Your identity. Period.
No platform can lock you out. No algorithm can silence you. No company can take your content hostage.

Core Principles

  • Self-Sovereign Identity: You control your cryptographic keys, therefore you control your identity.
  • Database as Source of Truth: Your writing lives in your database (SQLite), HTML is just a broadcast copy.
  • Decentralized Publishing: Publish to GitHub Pages, IPFS, RSS, Email, Substack, Medium - all from one interface.
  • Open Source & MIT Licensed: The core is free forever. No vendor lock-in, no proprietary formats.
  • Write Once, Publish Everywhere: One interface to rule them all. Write in Soulfra Studio, broadcast everywhere.

The Technology

Soulfra is built with:

  • Python + Flask - Local-first admin panel
  • SQLite - Your personal database (your wallet)
  • Git + GitHub Pages - Static site hosting & version control
  • Ollama AI - Local AI for content generation & research
  • IPFS - Decentralized content distribution
  • Syncthing - Multi-device sync (laptop + phones)

Philosophy: Like Bitcoin, But for Publishing

Bitcoin showed us that we don't need banks to control our money. Soulfra shows us that we don't need platforms to control our voice.

Bitcoin: Your wallet holds your private keys. Transactions are broadcast to the network.

Soulfra: Your database holds your content. HTML files are broadcast to the web.

The infrastructure is decentralized. The keys are yours. The content is yours. The future is self-sovereign.

Get Started

Soulfra is self-hosted and open source. The code is available on GitHub, and you can run it on your own machine.

No sign-ups, no cloud dependencies, no subscription fees. Just you, your machine, and your voice.
