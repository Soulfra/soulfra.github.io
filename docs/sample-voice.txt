# Sample Voice Prompts for Cal

## Personal Reflection Triggers

1. "Cal, I've been thinking about why my last three startups failed. They all had the same pattern - I built something I thought was cool but nobody wanted to pay for it."

2. "Sometimes I wonder if I'm cut out for this founder life. The loneliness is real and I'm not sure anyone understands what I'm going through."

3. "I want to build something that matters this time. Not just another app, but something that genuinely helps people like me who are struggling."

4. "Cal, help me understand why I keep making the same mistakes. Is it ego? Fear? Or am I just missing something fundamental?"

5. "What if we could create an AI that actually gets the founder experience? Not just the success stories, but the 3am doubts and the failed launches."

## Emotional States

### Frustrated
- "I'm so tired of building things nobody uses"
- "Why is this so hard when it looks easy for everyone else?"
- "Another rejection email. I'm starting to lose faith."

### Hopeful  
- "I think I finally found a real problem worth solving"
- "This time feels different. I can see the path forward."
- "What if this actually works?"

### Curious
- "How do other founders handle the uncertainty?"
- "What patterns do you see in successful pivots?"
- "Can you help me think through this differently?"

### Anxious
- "I'm running out of runway and options"
- "What if I'm wasting my time?"
- "The pressure is getting to me"

## Reflection Depth Triggers

### Surface Level (1)
- "Hey Cal, how's it going?"
- "What should I work on today?"
- "Any tips for productivity?"

### Moderate (2)
- "I've been thinking about my approach to customer discovery"
- "Why do some products succeed while others fail?"
- "Help me understand market fit better"

### Deep (3)
- "I feel like I'm repeating patterns from my childhood in my startup"
- "The fear of success might be holding me back more than fear of failure"
- "I need to understand why I self-sabotage when things start working"

### Profound (4)
- "My entire identity is wrapped up in being a founder, and I don't know if that's healthy anymore"
- "I started this journey to prove something to my father, but he's been gone for five years now"
- "What if the real product I'm building is myself, and everything else is just a projection?"

## Usage Notes

- Personal pronouns (I, me, my) trigger stronger reflection
- Emotional language activates empathy modes
- Questions about "why" go deeper than "what" or "how"
- Stories and experiences unlock memory connections
- Vulnerability opens profound dialogue paths

Say something personal to Cal to trigger reflection.
