# Privacy Policy

*Last Updated: January 2024*

## The Irony Isn't Lost on Us

Yes, we're a privacy company with a privacy policy. Here's the shortest, clearest one you'll ever read.

## What We Collect

### To Run Your Account
- **Email**: So we can send you important stuff
- **Payment Info**: Handled by Stripe (we never see your card number)
- **Device ID**: To ensure only you can access your data

### To Improve Service
- **Usage Metrics**: How much storage, number of API calls (not what you store)
- **Error Logs**: When things break (no personal data included)
- **Feature Usage**: Which features you use (not how you use them)

## What We DON'T Collect

- **Your Protected Data**: We literally can't see it. That's the whole point.
- **Biometric Data**: Stays on your device, never sent to us
- **Browsing History**: We're not creepy
- **Location Data**: Don't know, don't care where you are

## How We Use It

**Your Email**:
- Send you receipts
- Security alerts
- Major service updates
- Nothing else (we hate spam too)

**Usage Data**:
- Keep the service running
- Plan infrastructure capacity
- Fix bugs
- Make Soulfra better

## Who We Share With

**Nobody.** 

Seriously. We don't sell, rent, or trade your information. Ever.

**Exceptions**:
- Stripe sees payment info (they're the processor)
- Law enforcement with a valid warrant (we'll fight unreasonable requests)
- If we're acquired (you'll have 30 days to export and delete your data)

## Your Rights

**Access**: Download all data we have about you anytime

**Correction**: Fix any incorrect information

**Deletion**: Delete your account and all associated data

**Portability**: Export everything in standard formats

**Object**: Opt out of any processing you don't like

## Data Storage

**Account Data**: Encrypted in our database (US-based)

**Your Protected Data**: 
- Fragmented using our patented process
- Distributed across multiple locations
- Encrypted with quantum-resistant algorithms
- Only you can reassemble it

## Security

We use:
- TLS 1.3 for all connections
- AES-256 for data at rest
- Quantum-resistant algorithms for protected data
- Regular security audits
- Bug bounty program

## Cookies

We use essential cookies only:
- Session management (keep you logged in)
- Security tokens (prevent attacks)
- User preferences (remember your settings)

No tracking cookies. No advertising cookies. No creepy stuff.

## Children

Soulfra is for users 13+. If you're under 13, please have a parent set up your account.

## International Users

We're US-based but welcome users worldwide. Your data is processed in the US with protections exceeding GDPR requirements.

## Changes

We'll notify you 30 days before any significant changes. Check the "Last Updated" date above.
